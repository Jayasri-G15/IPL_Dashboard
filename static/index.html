How to Read This Dashboard

Even if you've never watched cricket — you'll understand everything here!

01 Win Rate

How often a team wins. 60% means they win 6 out of every 10 matches. Simple! 🎯

02 Strike Rate

How fast a batter scores. SR of 150 means 150 runs per 100 balls faced. Higher = more aggressive! ⚡

03 Economy Rate

Runs a bowler gives per over (6 balls). 7.5 economy = 7.5 runs per over. Lower = better bowler! 🎳

04 Toss Win Rate

% of matches won by the team that won the coin toss. 50% = no edge, 60% = big advantage! 🪙

🪙 Toss Impact

Does the Coin Decide?

Analyzing how winning the toss affects match outcomes — and whether choosing to bat or field makes a difference.

🪙
What is a Toss?

Before every IPL match, both captains meet in the middle. The umpire flips a coin — whoever calls it right gets to CHOOSE: bat first (try to set a big score) or field first (try to bowl the other team out cheaply). This is the TOSS! If bars are near 50%, the toss doesn't really matter. If a bar is much higher — the toss winner had a real advantage!

Win Rate: Bat First vs Field First

Toss Edge Across All Seasons

🧠
Key Insight to Remember:

If the toss had ZERO effect, we'd expect exactly 50% win rate (pure luck). Anything significantly above 50% means the toss decision matters. In modern IPL, most teams prefer to FIELD first — because chasing a known target is psychologically easier than setting one! 🎯
